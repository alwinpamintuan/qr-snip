# QR Snip

Scan a QR code already visible in your browser without uploading a screenshot or reaching for your phone.

## Short description

Select, decode, and safely preview QR codes from the visible browser tab.

## Full description

QR Snip freezes the visible tab, lets you select one QR code, and decodes it locally on your device. Every result is previewed before any optional navigation. Suspicious link signals are explained directly, and screenshots or decoded values are never uploaded or stored.

Permissions are limited to temporary access after you click the toolbar action or use the configured shortcut.
